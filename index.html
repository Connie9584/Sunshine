Welcome to Sunshine, a community charity that is changing the world one smile at a time. We are not just a charity; we are a movement of people who care about each other and want to make a difference. We are here to bring joy and community spirit to everyone, especially those who feel lonely, isolated, or struggling with life’s challenges. We are here to connect, support, and empower each other, and to create positive change in our local community.
We offer a variety of opportunities and experiences for the community, from fun and engaging activities, programmes and events, to safe and supportive spaces, learning and growth opportunities, and positive and motivational role models. We want to help everyone discover their potential.
